Lager

Lager, the beer style almost everyone's familiar with, is known for its crispness and refreshing finish.

Ale

Ales are full-bodied with hints of fruit or spice and a hoppy finish. They're also known to quence a mean thirst.

Malt

Sweeth tooth? Malts, often containing notes of caramel, toffee, and nuts, are dark and sweet in flavor.

Stout

Stouts, owing their dark color to roasted barley, add depth and character to your glass. If in doubt, stout it out.
